# 4×4矩阵分块乘法 6种块级顺序详细示例

## 设定

A = 4×4，B = 4×4，C = 4×4

A:
| a0 | a1 | a2 | a3 |
|----|----|----|----|
| a4 | a5 | a6 | a7 |
| a8 | a9 | a10| a11|
| a12| a13| a14| a15|

B:
| b0 | b1 | b2 | b3 |
|----|----|----|----|
| b4 | b5 | b6 | b7 |
| b8 | b9 | b10| b11|
| b12| b13| b14| b15|

C:
| c0 | c1 | c2 | c3 |
|----|----|----|----|
| c4 | c5 | c6 | c7 |
| c8 | c9 | c10| c11|

## 6种块级顺序 + 元素级ijk

### 1️⃣ ijk
```
for ii in [0,2]:
  for jj in [0,2]:
    for kk in [0,2]:
      for i in [ii,ii+1]:
        for j in [jj,jj+1]:
          for k in [kk,kk+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

### 2️⃣ jik
```
for jj in [0,2]:
  for ii in [0,2]:
    for kk in [0,2]:
      for j in [jj,jj+1]:
        for i in [ii,ii+1]:
          for k in [kk,kk+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

### 3️⃣ kij
```
for kk in [0,2]:
  for ii in [0,2]:
    for jj in [0,2]:
      for k in [kk,kk+1]:
        for i in [ii,ii+1]:
          for j in [jj,jj+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

### 4️⃣ ikj
```
for ii in [0,2]:
  for kk in [0,2]:
    for jj in [0,2]:
      for i in [ii,ii+1]:
        for k in [kk,kk+1]:
          for j in [jj,jj+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

### 5️⃣ jki
```
for jj in [0,2]:
  for kk in [0,2]:
    for ii in [0,2]:
      for j in [jj,jj+1]:
        for k in [kk,kk+1]:
          for i in [ii,ii+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

### 6️⃣ kji
```
for kk in [0,2]:
  for jj in [0,2]:
    for ii in [0,2]:
      for k in [kk,kk+1]:
        for j in [jj,jj+1]:
          for i in [ii,ii+1]:
            c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * b[k*4+j]
```

---

## 转置B后的顺序

转置后，B_T[j*4+k] = B[k*4+j]，内层循环变为：
```
c[i*4+j] += a[i*4+k] * B_T[j*4+k]
```

只需把b[k*4+j]换成B_T[j*4+k]，其余循环结构不变。

---

## 说明

- 6种块级顺序决定了**小块的遍历顺序**，即B块被加载/复用/驱逐的全局轨迹。
- 元素级ijk（i→j→k）决定了**每个小块内的计算顺序**。
- 转置B后，所有顺序的内层只需把b[k*4+j]换成B_T[j*4+k]，可以让B的访问步长变为1。
- 性能差异的本质来自**块级顺序**，不是小块内的16步顺序。

如需每种顺序下16步的具体c?=a?*b?展开，也可补充。
